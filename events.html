Conference Presentation: Ulaga Sathanai Manadu

In this presentation delivered at the Ulaga Sathanai Manadu (World Record Conference), Dr. Kavimani explores the visual art elements in Thirukkural - interpreting classical Tamil couplets through artistic symbolism and design theory.

Art in Christianity-awareness

In this short talk, Dr. Kavimani shares insights on the art knowledge stream within Christianity, highlighting how visual traditions and symbolism are embedded in Christian heritage. He also discusses awareness-building through church art and its role in shaping cultural understanding.

Lecture: Tamil Historic Arts

In this session organized by Kaleidoscope, Madurai, Dr. Kavimani introduces key themes in Tamil historic arts, exploring ancient visual traditions, cultural symbolism, and their relevance in contemporary practice. The lecture was part of a multi-day educational series.
