About

Professor of Printmaking, researcher, and award-winning author. Passionate about Dalit art, Tamil heritage, and contemporary pedagogy.

Learn More

Publications

  • Memory Power Art Practices (2025)
  • Tamilians Drawing Traditions (2020)

Dr. Kavimani’s writings explore the intersections of art, identity, and pedagogy. His publications are widely cited in academic circles and have influenced contemporary printmaking practices across India.

Through his books and articles, he highlights the importance of Dalit art traditions, Tamil heritage, and the role of visual culture in social transformation.
