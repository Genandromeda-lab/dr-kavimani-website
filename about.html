Academic Journey

Dr. K. Kavimani has dedicated his career to Painting and Fine Arts, serving as a professor at the Government College of Fine Arts, Chennai. With over 15 years of teaching experience, he has nurtured countless artists, encouraging them to explore creativity as a transformative force.

Research & Publications

His works such as Memory Power Art Practices (2025) and Tamilians Drawing Traditions (2020) are widely cited in academic circles. His scholarship highlights the intersections of art, identity, and social justice.

Outreach & Legacy

Beyond academia, Dr. Kavimani has coordinated cultural festivals such as Chennai Sangamam and Art Market, bringing art into public spaces and communities. His legacy lies in bridging tradition and modernity, ensuring Tamil art and Dalit voices are celebrated globally.
